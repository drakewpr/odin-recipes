Chicken cordon bleu is a popular chicken dish that dates back to the 1940's. It likely came from a dish called veal kiev from paris in the 1800s. Now people around the world enjoy this incredibly delisious and filling dish. Even though this dish may take a little more effort and time it is more than worth it.
